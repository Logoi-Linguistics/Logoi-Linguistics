Logoi = Prolog & Lisp




Logoi is a minimalist "superdialect" (of the Prolog, Lisp, and JavaScript programming languages) designed for natural language programming and artificial ratiocination.




"The insufficient nature of language can often be a barrier to more free-flowing thought."

- Dr. L. L. Zamenhof, creator of Esperanto ("the Lisp of natural languages")




Press the [Escape] key at any time on this website to toggle "Macro Mode" either on xor off.




λόγοι is the Greek word for "words".
