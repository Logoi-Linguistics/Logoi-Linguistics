Logoi = Prolog ∧ Lisp




Logoi is a minimalist "superdialect" designed for natural language programming and artificial ratiocination.




"The insufficient nature of language can often be a barrier to more free-flowing thought."

- Dr. L. L. Zamenhof, creator of Esperanto ("the Lisp of spoken languages")




Press the [Escape] key at any time/place on this website to toggle "Macro Mode" either on xor off.




λόγοι is the Greek word for "words".
