Logoi = Prolog + Lisp




Logoi is, among other things, a minimalist "superdialect" of the Prolog and Lisp programming syntaxes, designed for rapid natural language programming.




Press the [Escape] key at any time on this website to toggle "Macro Mode" either on xor off.




"The insufficient nature of language can often be a barrier to more free-flowing thought."

- Dr. L. L. Zamenhof, creator of Esperanto*


* the Lisp of human languages




λόγοι is the Greek word for "words".
